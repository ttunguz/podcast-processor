The AI Daily Brief is a weekly newsletter that explores the impact of artificial intelligence on various industries, with a focus on the human element of AI development and management.
**Key takeaways:**
* **Microsoft's annual work trend index** declared 2023 as the year of the **Frontier firm**, indicating a shift towards human-led, agent-operated AI.
* **AI roles are exploding**, with over 20,000 open AI roles currently.
* **The AI era is creating new roles** that are not easily defined, requiring new skills from employees.
* **Changing work landscapes** necessitate organizations to adapt their talent acquisition and development strategies.
* **Media scrutiny** surrounding AI and human talent can be intense.
* **The AI Daily Brief** aims to provide insights and perspectives on these changes.
**Additional points:**
* The Moderna HR tech example demonstrates the importance of combining **tech and HR functions** to manage AI teams.
* **Hybrid work models** are becoming increasingly common, requiring organizations to develop strategies for managing AI in distributed teams.
* The AI Daily Brief is a valuable resource for anyone interested in understanding the impact of AI on the workforce.
